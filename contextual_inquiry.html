Contextual Inquiry

Roles: User Researcher, Interviewer, Consultant, Editor

Objective: The HUB is an opportunity center exclusively for College of Literature, Science, and the Arts (LSA). Key to the organization’s operation is the emphasis that a student’s major does not have to define their career path. My team and I evaluated the current communication practices in order to offer recommendations to improve engagement strategies for the Opportunity HUB Network.

Background research revealed the Opportunity HUB is a relatively new and growing office that aims to help students at the College of Literature, Sciences and Arts (LSA) to connect the skills they learn in their liberal arts education to their future careers. However, they are challenged by the breadth of the LSA college and the fields LSA students pursue since it serves more than 85 majors and 100 minors.

Using an organizational chart of the office we interviewed seven HUB team members. Unique semi-structured interview protocols were created for each interviewee to guide exploration of their unique knowledge and experiences with the HUB.

  1. Through the interviews researchers gained a holistic view of:
  2. How the HUB is marketed
  3. students currently engage with content
  4. How coaching sessions are booked and conducted
  5. Students’ understanding of the HUB and their services.
